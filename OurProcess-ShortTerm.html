GOALS DEVELOPMENT
The rental journey begins by fully understanding your goals and desired outcomes for your rental property. Over coffee, we'll work with you to identify when you'd like to rent your property, for how long you'd like to rent it, and most importantly why you want to rent and to whom.
PROPERTY ASSESMENT
We do a thorough walk-through of your property with you to identify its key selling points and those one of a kind characteristics renters will fall in love with during their next adventure. We also do an analysis of comparable properties and local community assets to ensure your home is the best represented listing online.
PROFILE CONSTRUCTION
Every property has a history and we love telling those stories to your renters. We work hard to build a compelling profile of your property, including important community, regional, and site specific highlights in an effort to help interested renters visualize themselves spending the next week or weekend at your property.
PHOTOGRAPHY & STAGING
Beginning first with staging the site, we capture each room and outdoor setting in a manner that welcomes renters in and encourages them to call your property home. We try to capture all images in one shoot, however, multiple trips may be required to ensure we effectively convey the beauty of your listing in all conditions.
LISTING & MARKETING
We specialize in marketing your property across the top listing services—Airbnb, VRBO, Homeaway, Flipkey— saving you the hassle and ensuring your vacancy rate is kept low, thus maximizing revenue generation. We'll identify top keywords for your property and work in a coordinated effort to ensure the highest reasonable ranking on the many different travel platforms.
RENTAL MANAGMENT
Herding cats while juggling plates could be considered one of our specialties when it comes to managing the customer experience before, during, and after rental. We help guests day and night get settled, secured, and booked not once but for multiple repeat visits by efficiently handling all communications, question answering, and guest logistics during their stay.
LISTING OPTIMIZATION
We study who's renting your property, why they're renting it, and what they'd like to see improved so that your listing becomes the most welcoming experience for every traveler. Each of our managed listings is part of a rigorous data capture process we use to ensure the optimization of pricing, messaging, and a renter communication.
